About US

FreshConnect is an online B2B marketplace for businesses dealing in Fresh Produce.
It facilitates business-to-business sales of highly perishable commodities like fresh fruits and vegetables.
It brings traders, wholesalers, retailers, importers and food processors in India onto a single platform.
Focussed at digitizing the agri supply chain, our vision is to be the Earth’s largest online exchange of Fresh Produce.

How We Work

FreshConnect mobile app allows traders to list their stock online in easy guided steps. A trader exploring the online marketplace, communicates directly with respective owners of the shortlisted stocks, fixes deals and finally places the order in ecommerce fashion. Our tech enabled handling and transport services fire up then.
